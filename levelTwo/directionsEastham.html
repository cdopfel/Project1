“Get On Bike Path In Eastham”

Bike Path

Go over one of the Cape Cod Bridges: the Sagamore Bridge or the Bourne Bridge. You will be driving on Route 6 all the way. When you approach the Rotory, head North towards Provincetown. Eastham is the first town North; Orleans would be south. Notice the Visitors Center. A great pit stop; extremely clean and 'dry' bathrooms. Continue into Wellfleet and you will see a sign on the right for Marconi Beach. You can drive down to the beach parking lot and then ride back up the smooth quiet road which is about 1.5 miles. This makes sense for me because I always spend time on the ocean as well so I pay to park or have the season pass. Or you can turn on ?? street and there is a small parking lot where many cyclists get on. There is no fee to park. placeholder
